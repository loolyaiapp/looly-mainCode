Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. What We Collect

When you purchase a license or sign in, we collect your email address. We also log anonymous download counts and app usage events (questions asked, trial status) with no personally identifiable information attached.

2. How We Use It

Your email is used solely to deliver your license key and send account-related notifications. We do not sell, share, or market to your email address.

3. Payments

Payments are processed by Razorpay. We do not store your card details. Razorpay's privacy policy governs payment data handling.

4. AI Queries

Questions you ask are sent to Groq's API for processing. They are not stored on our servers. Refer to Groq's privacy policy for how they handle inference data.

5. Cookies & Storage

We use browser localStorage to store your session token and license cache on your device. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

6. Data Retention

License records are retained for as long as your subscription is active plus 1 year for billing disputes. Anonymous analytics are deleted after 90 days.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing us at support@looly.app.

8. Contact

Questions? Email support@looly.app
